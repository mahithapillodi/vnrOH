About VNRVJIET

VNR Vignana Jyothi Institute of Engineering and Technology is an autonomous institute sponsored by Vignana Jyothi Society which began functioning from the academic year 1995-96. The society started with a motto 'TAMASOMA JYOTIRGAMAYA' which means, "From the darkness, lead me to light". It started with an aspiration to provide purposeful and quality education. UGC and AICTE recognised VNRVJIET as a College with Potential for Excellence and UGC has granted extension of AUTONOMOUS STATUS to VNRVJIET for the next 10 years. All the UG and PG programs in the institute are accredited by the NAAC with 'A++' grade. Based on a synthesis of direct experience, leading edge thinking and ancient wisdom, it taps into 'deeper levels of LEARNING for discovering new possibilities'. The Management of VNRVJIET believes that by being research oriented, by providing world class infrastructure and by adapting a strategic and practical approach towards teaching by experienced faculty, the institute differentiates itself from other institutions and joins the league of EXCLUSIVE AND PREFERRED INSTITUTIONS providing a value-sensitive workforce and leaders of tomorrow.

About OPEN HOUSE

A unique platform initiated by VNRVJIET that emphasizes on showcasing the innovative ideas of the faculty and students.

Young minds can exhibit their thoughts in the form of project demonstrations and poster presentations.

The themes encompass various domains of engineering and multidisciplinary projects.

Open house advocates the very thought “Necessity is the mother of innovation”. The various projects depicted would serve to bridge the gap between idea and implementation from a prototype to a real-time project.

This expo not only portrays the ideas but also helps them to reach the industrialists. It also serves as a connecting link between the innovators and the entrepreneurs.
